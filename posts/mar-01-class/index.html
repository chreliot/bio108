Dimensions of identification

Learning species (for quiz next week)

We’re continuing finish waterbirds with grebes and cormorants, and starting diurnal raptors—Accipitridae hawks and other families. Required species for this week are:

  1. Pied-billed Grebe (Podicipedidae)
  2. Horned Grebe (Podicipedidae)
  3. Double-crested Cormorant (Phalacrocoracidae)
  4. American Coot (Rallidae)
