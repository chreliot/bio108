May 3 Lab

Plan

We’re planning to go to Jamaica Bay Wildlife Refuge (eBird hotspot) for an extended trip, leaving at 8 from the regular place and returning about 12:30. See the illustrated checklist for an idea about what’s possible there for the first week of May. Songbirds are towards the bottom of that list. It should be awesome.

We will park at and start from the Visitor’s Center, where we may need to fill out permits for you if it is open. My plan is that we walk north through the woods first, looking for perching birds, and then continue counter-clockwise around the West Pond loop. Time permitting, we’ll cross Cross Bay Blvd and walk up to Big John’s Pond for the blind that looks across to the Barn Owl box.

Expected conditions

The trails at Jamaica bay are variously pebbled, grass, or dirt. We will not be going out on the mud. And it looks like we’re good for weather! As of Thursday the forecast is “Cloudy in the morning with scattered thunderstorms developing later in the day.”
