Feb 15 Lab

As of Thursday noon, this page is still evolving, as I wait for more info about the van, etc.

Plan

If we have the van (currently being repaired):

  1. Oyster Bay Marine Center (Sagamore Yacht Club) to look for waterfowl, targeting the rare in New York Pacific Loon that has been there for almost two weeks. eBird hotspot page, Google Maps
  2. Francis Pond Preserve just west of there to look for woods and pond birds, eBird hotspot page, Google Maps.

If we don’t have the van:

We will walk around the Hofstra campus and see what birds are living right around us, learning some common species.

Expected conditions

Cool temperatures forecast (actual 43°F; feels like 36°) with wind and possible, occasional showers. We will be on pavement, maybe grass, and probably trail. Any of those could be a little wet. Remember to dress warmly, but probably also with a rain shell in case rain pops up?
