Lab Apr 5

Among the early migrants, we’ll look for arriving Night Herons, Great and Snowy Egrets, and Greater Yellowlegs. We’ll also look for a Monk Parakeet colony, and think about their ecology and history. We’ll keep an eye out for other recent arrivals, like Osprey and Killdeer and ducks moving through, like Green-winged Teal.
