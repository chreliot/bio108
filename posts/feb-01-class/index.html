Feb 01 Class

We will meet in our Friday classroom, which is different from both your Monday classroom and our Friday lab space. It is 103 Brower (campus map location).

Learning species (for quiz next week)

  1. American Robin
  2. Northern Mockingbird

Also, learn the vocabulary terms on the distributed sheet, to be able to recognize where they go on a picture of a bird. You can use the bird topography diagrams in your Sibley book to study this (and you can find alternative diagrams online). The terms for this week (same as the sheet I handed out) are:

Not feathers

  • bill
    • upper mandible
    • lower mandible
  • eye
  • tarsus
  • toes

Feather groups

  • crown
  • supercilium
  • orbital feathers
  • lores
  • supraloral
  • eyeline
  • auricular (= cheek)
  • malar
  • chin
  • throat
  • breast
  • sides
  • flanks
  • belly
  • vent
  • undertail coverts
  • nape
  • scapulars
  • mantle
  • tail
