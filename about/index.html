Using this site

This site is for students in Bio 108 and 217 “Ornithology” (Spring 2019) at Hofstra University. It’s for my Friday class and lab sections of the course.

In addition to this site, I also use the course’s Blackboard site, which is available through the password-protected Hofstra Portal (linked above). The reason to put content in both places is that this site is easy for you to access and for me to publish to, while the Blackboard site is good for hosting password-protected information like grades.

Site structure and contents

In addition to resources and general instructions, this site will mainly include pages with specific information for each Friday class and trip. All the pages on the site are linked from the home page or along the top of each page.

Share things

Past students have shared things that are fun for everyone to see, like photos they took, science articles they found, bird videos, etc. It’s very easy for me to post links here or even embed things.
