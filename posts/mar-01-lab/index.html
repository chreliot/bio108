Mar 01 Lab

If you haven’t read the Field Trip Instructions and Weekly Checklist Assignment pages yet, please do!

Plan

We’ll deploy our diving-duck and related waterfowl skills and keep an eye out for winter perching birds and hawks. New hawks for us would include Cooper’s Hawk, Sharp-shinned Hawk, and Northern Harrier. We’ll start by scanning Jones Bay from the Jones Beach Coast Guard Station. Horned Grebes should be peaking about now, for instance. After that we will probably check for perching birds and hawks around the west end median and roadside, with possibly short follow up stops at Theodore Roosevelt Nature Center and Jones Beach Field 10.
