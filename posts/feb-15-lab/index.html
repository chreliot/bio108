Feb 15 Lab

Update: the van is apparently repaired, so we’re planning to use it. But if anyone prefers to travel to the first site on your own—Google Maps locations are below—and meet us there without coming to Hofstra, please contact me about that.

Plan

  1. Oyster Bay Marine Center (Sagamore Yacht Club); target species: bay waterfowl, especially the rare in New York Pacific Loon that has been there for almost two weeks, but also Bufflehead, mergansers, scaup, gulls. eBird hotspot page, Google Maps
  2. Francis Pond Preserve just west of there; target species: suburban woods birds like Black-capped Chickadee, Tufted Titmouse, nuthatches, woodpeckers, finches, and winter pond birds like Gadwall, Northern Pintail. eBird hotspot page, Google Maps.

Expected conditions

Cool temperatures forecast (actual 43°F; feels like 36°) with wind and possible, occasional showers. Remember to dress warmly, but probably also with a rain shell in case forecast possible rain pops up?

Yacht club and Roosevelt Memorial Park area is pavement and maybe grass; Francis Pond Preserve is probably dirt trail. Any of those could be a little wet.
