Observed Species

This is a list, in taxonomic order, of all the species we’ve observed on our lab trips this semester, up to April 26 (with two trips left to go!). Not everyone saw all the birds; this includes all the species that I observed and was able to point out to you, so that at least some students were able to observe them.

With two trips left to go, I count 103 species. I think only one is “heard-only”—Yellow Warbler—and we will almost certainly see those at JBWR.

(If you want a list more customized to what you observed, you can look at your eBird page. If eBird shows anything you didn’t observe, you have the option of deleting it from your list without affecting the group’s list.)
